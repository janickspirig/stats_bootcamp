## ‚ö†Ô∏è Areas for Improvement

### 1. **Missing Semester Quiz Collections**

**Gap:** Unlike the CS Bootcamp (which has FS_22 through FS_25 quiz folders), this statistics bootcamp lacks semester-specific practice exams.

**Impact:** We HSG students rely heavily on past exams (Altklausuren) for preparation. Having quizzes organized by semester (HS_24, FS_25, etc.) would be invaluable.

**Recommendation:** Create `quizzes/HS_24/`, `quizzes/FS_25/` folders with 10-12 quiz files each, mimicking the CS Bootcamp structure.

### 2. **Limited Python Integration**

**Current:** Basic "Excursus: Stats in Python" exists, but it's minimal.

**Opportunity:** As business students, we need to bridge Excel (familiar) to Python/R (professional). More optional Python snippets in formula pages would be helpful for those wanting to go beyond Excel.

**Business Case:** Show how `scipy.stats.ttest_ind()` compares to manual t-test calculations.

### 3. **Video Explanations**

**Gap:** The CS Bootcamp has embedded videos for complex topics.

**Need:** Topics like Central Limit Theorem and hypothesis testing would benefit from 3-5 minute explanatory videos. Our generation learns well from visual explanations.
