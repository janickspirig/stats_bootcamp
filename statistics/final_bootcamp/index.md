## Exam relevance & typical tasks (what you must do by hand)

You should be able to do these quickly and cleanly (formula ‚Üí substitution ‚Üí answer ‚Üí interpretation):

- Build calculation tables (means, deviations, squares, frequency tables)
- Standardize with z-scores and read the z-table (including symmetry/complement)
- Pick the right distribution (Binomial vs Poisson vs Hypergeometric; Normal/Exponential)
- Construct confidence intervals for mean and proportion
- Execute hypothesis tests using the **same 5-step template** every time
- Interpret outputs in one business sentence (what decision does this support?)
