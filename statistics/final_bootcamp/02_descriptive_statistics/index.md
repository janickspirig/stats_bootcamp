After completing this module, you will be able to:
- Calculate and interpret measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode)
- Calculate and interpret measures of dispersion (variance, standard deviation, MAD, IQR)
- Understand and interpret quartiles and box plots

- Build and interpret basic frequency tables (relative and cumulative) for grouped data (exam-style)
